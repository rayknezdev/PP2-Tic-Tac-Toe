TIC-TAC-TOE

Rules:

  • Decide which player is going first and will be player 'X'
  • Using the cursor, click on any blank tile to place your marker
  • The game will show it is the second player's turn. They now place their marker on a blank tile
  • Keep taking turns, placing your tiles until one player has 3 markers of their own in a row, or until it is a draw
  • Note: A player wins by getting 3 of their tiles in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally
